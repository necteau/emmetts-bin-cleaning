# Reference Research — Emmett’s Mobile Bin Cleaning Service

Reviewed examples:

- Bright Bins — best overall reference: direct problem headline, playful memorable tone, clear plans, before/after proof.
- Wash Bins — useful technical proof: cleaned after pickup, reminder flow, hot-water/pressure-wash/sanitize/deodorize sequence, clear pricing cards.
- Bubble Binz — strong “Step 1 / Step 2” clarity and route/ZIP workflow.
- Epic Bin Cleaning — strong promo/CTA energy and confidence framing.
- Bin Daddy / Liberty Bins — useful family/health/service-area framing, but less visually distinctive from fetched text.

Chosen “best” model to beat: Bright Bins. It has the clearest market fit: instantly names the disgusting problem, makes the service feel simple, and uses a playful brand personality. Emmett’s version borrows the strategy, not the copy/assets: stronger visual design, original generated images, bigger local-young-operator energy, 3D cards, motion, pricing, process, and quote capture.
